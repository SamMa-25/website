Urban Design

Pvd West End Container Housing Project - A comprehensive urban design process that prioritizes community engagement, sustainable practices, and adaptive reuse. This project demonstrates how hands-on physical modeling and iterative design methodology can create resilient communities through thoughtful integration of housing, production, and community spaces.

Modular Design Components

The grey color indicates concrete material used exclusively on the ground floor, providing structural stability and defining the foundational layer of the housing complex. The design proposes innovative 3D-printed concrete construction for transition spaces and common areas within the community housing complex, enabling rapid construction while maintaining architectural quality. This approach creates flexible, adaptable spaces that serve as social connectors between private living areas. Single and double unit private living spaces are represented by the light beige container-shaped housing, primarily located on the second floor to ensure privacy and optimal natural lighting. The modular system allows for various unit configurations, accommodating different family sizes and housing needs while maintaining consistent design language throughout the development.
