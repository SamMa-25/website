Project Details

This project explores the intersection of computational design and karmic transformation. Through algorithmic processes, the architecture emerges and evolves, creating spaces that respond to human experience and environmental conditions.

Process

The design process began with parametric modeling using Grasshopper and Rhino. The algorithm was designed to respond to site conditions, program requirements, and environmental factors, creating a form that emerges through iterative transformation.

Technology

  • Rhino + Grasshopper for parametric modeling
  • 3D printing for prototyping
  • Digital fabrication for final components
  • Interactive visualization for user feedback
