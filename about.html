About

Architectural Designer

I am a passionate architectural designer who views architecture as a karmic process of transformation and emergence. My work explores how spaces can evolve, adapt, and transform through time, creating environments that respond to and shape human experience.

Based in New York and Toronto, I bring a unique perspective to architectural design through my concentration in technology, computation, and digital media. I believe that architecture is not static, but a living process that emerges through the interaction of form, function, technology, and human consciousness.
